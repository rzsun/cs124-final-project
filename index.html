Introduction

Related individuals share DNA. Siblings on average share ~50% DNA because they inherit many of the same SMPs from their parents. By comparing the SNPs of two individuals, we can reasonably estimate whether they are related or not. This project will use two algorithms, a baseline algorithm as well as a improved algorithm to determine if two individuals are siblings given their SNPs.

Goals

To create and implement an algorithm that can determine whether individuals are related given their SNP to a reasonable degree of accuracy. Also to learn more about the application of computer science on the field of genetics.
